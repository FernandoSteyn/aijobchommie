# AI Job Chommie PWA - Wireframes & Design Specifications

## Design System
- **Primary Colors**: Neon Cyan (#00FFFF), Neon Pink (#FF00FF), Neon Green (#00FF00)
- **Background**: Deep Black (#000000) with gradient overlays
- **Typography**: Futuristic fonts with glow effects
- **3D Elements**: Floating cards, rotating icons, parallax scrolling
- **Animations**: Smooth transitions, pulse effects, morphing shapes
